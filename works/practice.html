Random Story #1: The Flower Maiden

Once upon a time in a far away land there lived a woman with beauty beyond imagination. She spent her waking days growing various plants in her magnificent garden.

Plant Type Examples Growing Season
Flowers Roses, Lilies, Tulips Spring
Fruits Apples, Berries, Citrus Summer
Vegetables Carrots, Lettuce, Tomatoes Fall

One day, she started having painful headaches and she started coughing out leaves and petals.

A magical garden similar to the one tended by the Flower Maiden

"Oh good heavens! Why must thy child bear such pain!
Why must thou bestow such sorrow on such a poor soul?
I pray that thou would bestow me mercy from this cruel fate."

Upon hearing such a sorrowful prayer filled with sincerity, their Lord spoke to them.

"My good child, I have seen your hard work and your sincerity.
For your earnest efforts, I shall grant you the mercy you seek.
On the night of the third day, all of your sufferings would come to an end."

The words her lord spoke before their voice vanished with the howls of the wind.
Bearing with the pain of her suffering, she continued to tend to her garden.
The more she moved, the more petals and leaves she coughed up.

The morning of the 3rd day arrived and she was unable to rise from her bed.
Flowers and leaves surrounded her body, and the once beautiful maiden vanished.
All that was left of her were the multiple flowers and leaves that beautifully bloomed on her bed.
